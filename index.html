About me

Hey there! I'm Raihaan — a web designer and software engineer with a deep love for turning ideas into immersive digital experiences. My journey began with curiosity and a keyboard, and today it’s fueled by a passion for clean code, cinematic visuals, and meaningful storytelling. I specialize in crafting responsive websites, debugging complex logic circuits, and building games that spark joy — from Snake to Subway Surfer clones. But beyond the tech, I believe design is emotional. It’s about creating interfaces that feel intuitive, visuals that resonate, and systems that earn trust. Whether I’m experimenting with Java, C#, or Linux environments, or sketching out UI/UX flows, I bring both precision and personality to the table. I’m also someone who values deep connections — especially with my best friend Anfas, whose loyalty and laughter inspire me daily. If you're looking for someone who codes with heart and designs with soul, you're in the right place. Let’s build something unforgettable.
